How we work

From first conversation
to pilot: 90 days.

A structured process to go from an ill-defined industrial challenge to a pilot with clear metrics and an assigned team.

PHASE 01

Discovery

Strategic conversation with the director and area leads to frame the challenge in terms of AIMS capabilities.

DURATION · 2 WEEKS
PHASE 02

Scoping

Joint definition of technical hypothesis, impact metric, pilot scope and assigned research team.

DURATION · 4 WEEKS
PHASE 03

Pilot

Execution in plant or field with a mixed AIMS + industrial partner team. Real data, iterative cycle, live metrics.

DURATION · 12+ WEEKS
PHASE 04

Scale

Transfer, scaling to multiple sites, transition to a multi-year program and joint publications.
