Director · AIMS Institute

Josué C. Velázquez Martínez, PhD

Director, AI for Manufacturing and Supply Chain Institute, Tecnologico de Monterrey
Research Affiliate, MIT Center for Transportation and Logistics

Dr. Velázquez Martínez leads AIMS at the intersection of artificial intelligence, manufacturing systems and supply chain operations. He spent over a decade as Research Scientist and Lecturer at the MIT Center for Transportation & Logistics, leading its SCM master's program, the SCALE Latin America Network, and the Sustainable Supply Chain Lab. He is the founder of the MIT LIFT Lab, now expanding at Tec de Monterrey, which applies AI to the supply chain challenges of micro and small enterprises in emerging markets. He holds a PhD in Industrial Engineering from Tec de Monterrey, graduated with highest distinction and recipient of the 2014 National Doctoral Dissertation Award.
